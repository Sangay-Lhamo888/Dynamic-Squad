A brief experience of designing a soap using tinkercad Our group used the website called Tinkercad, a powerful online design tool, to create a unique and intricate design for a flowery shaped soap. Despite being the last group to finish, we dedicated ourselves to perfecting our design, and the end result was truly worth the effort. Tinkercad provided us with a user-friendly interface and a wide range of tools and features that allowed us to bring our creative vision to life. We were able to experiment with different shapes, sizes, and patterns, and the flexibility of Tinkercad enabled us to refine our design until it met our exact specifications. The collaborative nature of the application also allowed us to work seamlessly as a team, sharing ideas and making adjustments in real-time. Overall, our experience with Tinkercad was both educational and enjoyable, as it not only allowed us to explore the world of 3D design but also taught us valuable skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and attention to detail. We are proud of our accomplishment and grateful for the opportunity to work with such a powerful and intuitive design tool.
